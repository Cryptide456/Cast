About Us

The proud founders of this innovative and successful business, Nolan and Nathan pride themselves on their ability to sustain a healthy and productive relationship with their customer base. Both graduated from the same Highschool named West Springfield High School. Together they had a dream to make the rare fish market accessible to the average person as well as to supply chefs and aquarists with such elusive fish. Self made millionaires, off of their legal crypto schemes they were able to open Cast. The co-founder Nolan states" It's a great honor and privilege to be able to fill the gap of the rare fish industry as well as to be connected to so many Business such as FOTgo". Nathan from time to time also says the line " Don't get caught on the hook of unfresh seafood." And unfresh seafood is something which Cast strives to get rid off with its easy access and other partnership with AcornAir.
